How it works

  1. Pick difficulty and target size.
  2. Hit as many targets as you can before the timer ends.
  3. Accuracy and streaks boost your score. Misses hurt.

Tip: On mobile, use your thumb; on desktop, keep the mouse low‑latency (gaming mode helps).

About

Aim Master 2025 is a lightweight aim trainer focused on latency and consistency. No frameworks, no build tools — just clean, readable code you can study and extend.

  • Mobile + desktop responsive
  • Local high score and stats
  • Accessible controls and ARIA labels
  • MIT licensed — open source
